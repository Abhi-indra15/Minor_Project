Frequently Asked Questions

Q.1 What is learners ?

Ans. Learner is an online platform which is publicly available for all to manage their records and interact with people of their choice. It is an online community to learn together and share together.

Q.2 How can I register in learners ?

Ans. It is very simple and time saving just go to our website Learners and you can find a button named register on the top right corner just click on it and provide some necessary information like your username and create your login I'd and password and you are registered. In other case if you want to login with some other trustable site it requires just a click and you are registered.

Q.3 Who can register in learners ?

Ans. Anyone its not for any specific category. If you want to learn something new or share your learning you are welcomed. Even if you just want a space to keep record of your information you can register in it.

Q.4 How learners can help me ?

Ans. It can help you in managing your records. You can find a guidance and opportunities here . we also provide some enhanced features to save your time like notification and personal profile manager. Also we provide you an interface to connect with others .

Q.5 Can I trust Learners ?

Ans. Yes, you can this is a platform which is created for users comfort only. And we don't share anything to anyone unless you wanted to do so.
